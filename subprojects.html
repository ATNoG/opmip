Extensions for Distributed Mobility Management (DMM)

Leveraging on the flexible nature of OPMIP's software architecture, towards the implementation of upcoming evolutions to IP mobility management. Concretely, Distributed Mobility Management explores dynamic LMA/MAG deployments that go beyond the current PMIP centralised design, facilitating and optimising network-based mobility management procedures.
